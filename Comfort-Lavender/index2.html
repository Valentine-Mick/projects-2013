FAQ

  • What is the duration of the competition?

    The competition runs from the 05 June - 31 July 2013

  • For the best experience, which web browser should I be using?

    We recommend you update your browser to the latest version. Not only will you have a better experience Showing Your Love with Lavender, doing so keeps you secure online. Google Chrome is our favourite.

  • Nothing happens when I click Play, why can't I download the game?

    If you are trying to play the game at work, please refer to your company internet guidelines as they could have a firewall in place to prevent you from playing.

  • Is this safe for my computer?

    Yes. The game has been thoroughly tested and does not contain any malicious software that can harm your computer.

  • I can't find all five bottles, where are they?

    Make sure that you have the game installed. The bottles are spread out all over the internet, you should however be able to easily find at least one bottle a day. In a week you could easily have an entry into winning your second honeymoon with all 5 bottles collected.
    Good luck.

  • Will the game work on my phone or tablet?

    This game is designed to be played on desktop and laptop computers only.

  • Do I need Facebook to play the game?

    Yes. In order for you to save the bottles you collect, you need to connect with Facebook.

  • How do I stop playing?

    Click here to stop playing.

  • Where do I get my vouchers from?

    Once you have collected your 3rd and 5th bottle, your vouchers can be found by clicking the 'Prizes' tab above.
    NB: Vouchers cannot be redeemed via Tweeting for a bottle - only the 3rd and 5th bottles found via browsing the web will reward you.

  • Where can I find the clues?

    The clues can be found on the Comfort Zone South Africa Facebook page www.facebook.com/ComfortZoneSouthAfrica or you can find your daily clue by clicking the 'Clues' tab above.
