  • Germs in the home

    Germs are on a mission to make our homes their own. Our ‘safe havens’ are packed with paradise spots for thousands of nasty parasites, so keeping all surfaces properly cleaned and disinfected is vital to guarding your family from harm.

    Keep your HOME hygienically clean by following these simple tips:

    1 ILLNESS IN THE HOMEPrevent germs from spreading by cleaning surfaces that are touched i.e. floors, door handles and taps with diluted Domestos.
    2 PLASTIC GARDEN FURNITURETo remove ingrained dirt and restore original colour, wash with diluted Domestos, rinse and allow to dry.
    3 GLASS VASESAdd a few drops of Domestos into a glass vase, fill with water and allow to stand. Rinse and allow to dry.
    4 CUT FLOWERSAdd a few drops of Domestos to keep the water in your vase of flowers clean and odourless. Your flowers will remain fresh for longer.
    5 GARAGE FLOORS & DRIVEWAYSScrub with diluted Domestos to help remove grease and oil stains.
    6 BRICKWORKWash with diluted Domestos, rinse well and allow to dry.
    7 DRAINS & WASTE PIPESTo eliminate bad odours, squirt Domestos down the drain, leave for 10 minutes and flush with water.
    8 PATHS & PATIOSTo remove unsightly moss and leaf stains wash over with diluted Domestos. Leave for 10 minutes and wash away.
    9 MOPSTo get rid of smells and germs, soak in diluted Domestos for 10 minutes, rinse and allow to dry.
    10 CHILD’S POTTYWash potty with diluted Domestos, rinse and leave to dry.
    11 PLAY PENSWash plastic parts with diluted domestos, rinse and allow to dry.
    13 CHILDREN’S PLASTIC TOYSWipe with diluted Domestos, rinse well and allow to dry.
    14 PETS KENNELS & FOOD BOWLSTo keep hygienic, wipe down with diluted Domestos, leave for 10 minutes before rinsing well and drying.
    15 EXTERIOR RUBBISH BINSTo get rid of stains, smells and germs, squirt with Domestos around walls of bin, leave for 10 minutes and simply rinse off.
    16 KITTY LITTER TRAYWash litter tray with diluted Domestos, rinse and leave to dry.
